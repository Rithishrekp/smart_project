This is to certify that Mrs. Rithika Sharma, aged 34 years, daughter of Mr. Rajesh Sharma, residing at No. 18, Green Park Colony, Hyderabad, Telangana, PIN code 500081, was admitted to Apollo Hospitals, Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad, on 10 August 2025 with complaints of persistent high fever, cough, fatigue, and breathlessness for the past seven days. Upon admission, her vitals were recorded as blood pressure 130/85 mmHg, pulse rate 96 beats per minute, respiratory rate 22 breaths per minute, and oxygen saturation 93 percent on room air. The patient was subjected to diagnostic investigations including Complete Blood Count (CBC), Chest X-Ray, ECG, and RT-PCR for viral infections. Laboratory reports revealed hemoglobin level of 12.3 g/dL, white blood cell count of 11,500 per microliter, platelet count of 2,20,000 per microliter, and C-reactive protein level of 32 mg/L indicating moderate inflammation. The chest X-ray showed mild bilateral infiltrates consistent with viral pneumonia. The RT-PCR test returned positive for Influenza A (H1N1). ECG showed no abnormality. The patient was started on antiviral therapy with Oseltamivir 75 mg twice daily for 5 days, intravenous fluids, and supportive oxygen therapy through nasal cannula at 2 liters per minute. Paracetamol 650 mg was administered every 6 hours to control fever, and Vitamin C supplementation was prescribed to support recovery. She responded well to treatment, her oxygen saturation improved to 97 percent within 48 hours, and fever subsided gradually. The patient was discharged on 16 August 2025 with advice to continue oral medications for 5 more days, maintain proper hydration, follow a high-protein diet, and undergo follow-up review after 10 days at the hospital outpatient department. The discharge medications included Oseltamivir, Paracetamol, and Multivitamin supplements. The patient was advised strict home isolation for one week to prevent transmission. This report is issued on 16 August 2025 under the authority of Dr. Anil Reddy, Consultant Pulmonologist, Apollo Hospitals, Hyderabad.
